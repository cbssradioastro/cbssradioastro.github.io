ABOUT THE GROUP

The research group was fomed with the instaltion ofthe CBSS3.7m radio telescope in Augst of 2023. Thegroup is headed by the Director of the centre Dr. Okere B.I, Prof. Chibueze James and Dr. Obi A.I are the lead scientst.

Some staff of the radio astronomy department of CBSS are members of this resarch group.

Exampls of esearch conducted with the 3.7m radio telescope are:

  • mapping the distribution of neutral hydrogen in the Milky Way to derive the rotation curve.
  • Study the variability of nearby radio galaxies.
  • Monitor the HI intensity from nearby radio galaxy
  • Observe bright quasars to know when they launch jets

"The Centre for Basic Space Scienec (CBSS), Nsukka 3.7m radio telescope is meant to introduce scientists at CBSS, undergraduate and post graduate students of physics & astronomy departement University of Nigeria Nsukka (UNN), and other astronomy students in Nigeria to radio astronomy research.

Director CBSS: Dr. Okere B.I

Opening hours: Weekdays from 8am to 5pm.
